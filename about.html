About

The goal of this site is to display character information from the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen.

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The manga began serialization in Weekly Sh≈çnen Jump in March 2018 and quickly gained popularity.

The anime adaptation of Jujutsu Kaisen was produced by MAPPA and premiered in October 2020. The anime brought wider attention to the series due to its animation quality, fight choreography, and voice acting.

The story takes place in a world where cursed spirits are created from negative human emotions. Jujutsu sorcerers are trained to fight these curses using cursed energy and specialized techniques. The series follows Yuji Itadori as he becomes involved in the jujutsu world after encountering a powerful curse, alongside other students, teachers, and enemies.
